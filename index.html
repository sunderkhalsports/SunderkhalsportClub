About Us

Welcome to Sunderkhal Sports Club, where we believe in nurturing talent and fostering sportsmanship. We offer a range of sports activities for all ages and skill levels.

Our mission is to create a positive and inclusive environment for athletes to grow, compete, and enjoy their passion for sports.

Tournaments

Indoor Sports Tournaments

Join us for exciting tournaments in basketball, badminton, and table tennis. Our indoor tournaments are designed for both competitive and casual players.

Upcoming Events: Winter Championship, Indoor Cup.

Outdoor Sports Tournaments

Participate in our thrilling outdoor tournaments like cricket, football, and athletics. Experience the thrill of competition under the sun!

Upcoming Events: Summer League, Outdoor Challenge.

E-Sports Tournaments

Get ready for our E-Sports tournaments featuring popular games like FIFA, PUBG, and Call of Duty. Compete for glory and prizes!

Upcoming Events: E-Sports Championship, Monthly Tournaments.

Our Services

At Sunderkhal Sports Club, we offer a variety of services to support athletes and sports enthusiasts:

  • Coaching: Professional coaching for various sports.
  • Fitness Training: Personalized fitness programs to enhance performance.
  • Workshops: Skill development workshops for all age groups.
  • Health & Wellness: Nutrition and wellness programs to support your athletic journey.
