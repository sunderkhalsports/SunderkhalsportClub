About Us

We are a sports club dedicated to promoting sports in Sunderkhal. Our mission is to provide quality sports facilities for all.

Sunderkhal Sports Club offers training programs for youth, adults, and seniors. We specialize in football, cricket, and other community activities.

Our Services

We offer coaching, sports events, and facility rentals.

Our services include expert coaching in football and cricket, organizing sports tournaments, and renting sports equipment and grounds.
